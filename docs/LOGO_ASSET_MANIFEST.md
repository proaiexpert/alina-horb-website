## Usage rules

- Use `*-dark.png` on ivory and other light surfaces.
- Use `*-light.png` on dark or photographic surfaces only when contrast is sufficient.
- Preserve aspect ratio.
- Do not stretch, recolor, add shadows or crop the monogram independently.
- A true vector SVG may be prepared later only from an authentic editable source. Do not use automatic tracing as a replacement for the approved artwork.
